FAQ

Common Questions

Do you accept sponsored reviews?

No. Our editorial reviews are 100% independent. We do carry display advertising (clearly labelled) and earn affiliate commissions when readers sign up through our links, but these arrangements have no influence on our scores or recommendations. Our affiliate disclosure explains this in full.

Can I republish your content?

Short excerpts (up to 200 words) may be republished with attribution and a link back to the original article. Full reproduction requires written permission. Please contact us via the form above for licensing enquiries.

How do I submit my tool for review?

Use the contact form above and select "Suggest a tool for review". Include the tool name, URL, pricing, and why you think it's relevant for UK small businesses. We review all submissions but cannot guarantee publication.

I found incorrect information — how do I report it?

We take accuracy seriously. If you spot an error in any of our articles, please use the contact form with the subject "Feedback on an article" and we'll investigate and correct it promptly. Corrections are acknowledged at the bottom of the updated article.
