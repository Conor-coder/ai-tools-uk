Our Story

Built by UK Business Owners, for UK Business Owners

We got tired of reading AI reviews written by people who'd never run a small business. So we built AIToolsUK — honest, practical, and 100% independent.

Our Mission

Helping UK Small Businesses Compete with AI

The UK has 5.5 million small businesses. Most don't have a tech team, a six-figure marketing budget, or a dedicated operations manager. But in 2026, the right AI tools for small businesses can level the playing field — giving a one-person operation the capabilities of a 20-person agency.

Our mission is simple: test every relevant AI tool, cut through the hype, and tell you honestly which ones are worth your money and time — in plain British English.

We earn a small affiliate commission when you sign up through our links, but this never influences our scores or recommendations. We've given unfavourable reviews to tools with generous affiliate programmes, and glowing reviews to tools that don't pay us a penny. That's the deal.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

🔍

Rigorous Testing

We use every tool we recommend in a real business context for a minimum of 30 days before publishing a review. Screenshots and opinions aren't enough — we need real data.

🇬🇧

UK-Specific Context

We only recommend tools that work properly in the UK — correct currency formatting, UK GDPR compliance, VAT awareness, and integration with UK banking and accounting software.

💬

Plain English

No jargon, no buzzwords, no endless feature lists. We tell you exactly what a tool does, what it costs, who it's for, and whether you should bother — in plain British English.

🚫

No Sponsored Reviews

We do not accept payment for positive reviews. Affiliates are disclosed. Our editorial team has full independence from our commercial team. Scores are determined by testing, not invoices.
