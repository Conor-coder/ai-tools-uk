FAQ

Common Questions from UK Business Owners

Do I need technical skills to use AI tools?

No. The best AI tools for small business in the UK are designed for non-technical users. If you can use email and a smartphone, you can use Claude, Canva AI, or Tidio. Most tools offer guided setup and UK-based customer support.

Are AI tools GDPR-compliant for UK businesses?

Post-Brexit, UK businesses must comply with UK GDPR. All tools on our recommended list offer UK GDPR-compliant data processing agreements. Read our full GDPR guide for tool-by-tool details.

What's the best AI tool for a very small budget?

Start with Claude's free tier for writing and analysis, Canva AI's free plan for design, and Tidio's free chatbot for customer service. A powerful AI stack for under £30/month once you upgrade.

Will AI replace my employees?

AI tools are designed to augment your team, not replace it. The UK businesses seeing the best results use AI to handle repetitive, time-consuming tasks so their people can focus on strategy, relationships, and creative work.
